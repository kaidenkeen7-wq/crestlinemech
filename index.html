New installations carry a 2-year labor warranty from Crestline Mechanical Group, in addition to the manufacturer's equipment warranty — typically 5–10 years on parts depending on the brand and product line. Enrollment in our preventive maintenance program keeps manufacturer warranties valid and extends our labor coverage to 5 years.

Our average emergency response time across our service area is under 2 hours, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including holidays. On-call technicians are staged across The Woodlands, Conroe, and Magnolia. When you call our main line after hours, you reach a live dispatcher, not a voicemail.

Our standard plan includes two scheduled tune-ups per year (spring and fall), a 21-point system inspection, coil cleaning, filter replacement, refrigerant level check, and thermostat calibration. Contract customers also receive 15% off unscheduled service calls, priority emergency dispatch, and a detailed efficiency report after each visit.

In Texas, the minimum federally mandated SEER2 rating for new installations is 14.3 (equivalent to roughly SEER 15). For Houston's climate, we typically recommend 17–20 SEER2 for residential applications — the incremental cost pays back through utility savings in 4–6 years. Commercial systems are evaluated on a case-by-case basis using an energy model tied to your actual utility rates.

Yes, always. We pull all required mechanical permits before beginning work and schedule city or county inspections. This is non-negotiable — unpermitted HVAC work can void manufacturer warranties, create issues with homeowners insurance, and complicate property sales. Our crews are familiar with permit requirements across Montgomery County, Harris County, and all municipalities within our service area.

Standard emergency dispatch includes a flat after-hours fee applied to the diagnostic visit. Parts and labor are billed at normal rates — we do not apply after-hours premiums to repair work. Maintenance contract customers have the after-hours dispatch fee waived entirely. We'll always give you a full estimate before beginning any repair.

Our primary service area covers The Woodlands, Magnolia, Conroe, Spring, Tomball, Humble, Kingwood, and surrounding communities in Montgomery and Harris counties. We also serve commercial accounts in downtown Houston and the Energy Corridor. Call our office to confirm coverage for your specific location.
